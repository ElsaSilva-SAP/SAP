## ðŸ’¡ Part 2: What is Business Process Management?

### Simple Definition

**Definition:** Business Process Management (BPM) is the practice of designing, executing, monitoring, and optimizing business processes.

**In Simple Terms:** BPM is about:
- Understanding how work gets done
- Making processes better
- Managing processes effectively
- Improving continuously

**Think of it as:** Managing the "how" of business operations

### What is a Business Process?

**Definition:** A business process is a series of steps that accomplish a business goal.

**Examples:**
- Order to cash (selling products)
- Procure to pay (buying materials)
- Hire to retire (managing employees)
- Quote to cash (sales process)
